The Experience

The Anatomy of a Lamyat

1

Maguindanao Tinagtag

The heart of the Lamyat. Thousands of hand-woven rice flour strands creating a delicate, golden crunch that echoes Mindanao's soul.

2

Pure Ube Halaya

A thick layer of vibrant, locally-sourced ube jam. Creamy, earthy, and perfectly sweet.

3

Pillowy Marshmallow

A soft, gooey envelope that binds the crunch and the jam into one harmonious chew.

4

Toasted Polvoron Dusting

The final touch. A buttery, crumbly dusting that adds a hint of nostalgic merienda flavor.
